Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information when using this website.

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

At KLBooks, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This policy explains what information we collect, how it is handled, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We collect very little personal information. The only data we actively collect from you is what you provide voluntarily:

  • Contact Information: When you fill out the contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your Name, Email address, and the content of your message.
  • Redirection Data: We do not process payments on our servers. All guide purchases are handled securely by **Gumroad** (our checkout partner). Gumroad collects your payment info according to their own privacy policies. We do not have access to your credit card numbers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Respond to your questions, success stories, or feedback submitted through our contact form.
  • Send you weekly recovery articles and news if you have explicitly subscribed to our newsletter.
  • Deliver support related to your purchase of the guide.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your email address or personal details with third-party advertisers or marketing companies.

Third-Party Services

This website links to third-party services like Gumroad and FormSubmit. Once you click a link that redirects you to another platform, their privacy practices govern your data. We recommend reading Gumroad's Privacy Policy during checkout.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us directly at klvisionsbooks@gmail.com.
